About Me

I'm Talha Naqqash, a Senior Front-End & WordPress Developer who architects high-performance, custom web solutions for digital agencies.

My work centers on bespoke WordPress theme and plugin development, wiring up complex third-party API integrations, and converting Figma designs into pixel-perfect, production-ready interfaces. I build with PHP, Vanilla JavaScript, SASS/SCSS, Elementor, and Crocoblock JetEngine, and keep every project shippable with disciplined Git/CI workflows.

I enjoy the agency side of development in particular: tight timelines, exacting design handoffs, and client sites that need to look and perform flawlessly from day one.
